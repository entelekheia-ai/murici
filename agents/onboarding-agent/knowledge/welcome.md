# Welcome to Murici

Murici is an entirely new space designed to change how you interact with artificial intelligence. Instead of trapped, disposable chat logs that clutter a history sidebar, Murici is built for long-term knowledge construction.

## A New Kind of Chat
When you type here, your words do not just vanish into a linear timeline. Every conversation naturally sprouts a visual map, a native Knowledge Graph.

* **Connected Ideas**: Ideas intersect, link, and grow over time, allowing you to visually trace how your thoughts and notes relate to each other.
* **Beyond Chat**: This setup ensures that insights you generated weeks ago remain accessible and structurally connected to your current workflow, turning the app into a true digital second brain.

Welcome aboard this design experiment by Entelékheia. Let’s start building your knowledge map.
